Raw Data

I am a ninja, dressed as one. In a room of many guests, the queen stands. I am the only ninja in the room, guests being regular nobles. I kneel to the ground in front of queen and bow down low in a baby pose. The queen is pleased, my show of gratitude in a world where many have lost it. I swiftly walk to the door, put on my shoes (a little clumsly), and head to my pickup truck. In the truck, going in reverse, too fast, i break hard, i break even harder. the truck skids upon the dirt, i go off the edge of a cliff, the truck uncontrollably slides down the cliff's slope into the abyss. I consider jumping out of the window, but i remain in the truck, my tomb.
